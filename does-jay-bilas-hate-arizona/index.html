Does Jay Bilas Hate Arizona?

25/Mar 2015

Probably not. But he certainly doesn’t think Arizona would win in an Elite 8 rematch with Wisconsin (should both teams advance):

@JayBilas and @JeffGoodman went back and forth about which Sweet 16 teams are most ‘upset-proof’. In hindsight, they should have been a tad more explicit (i.e. Which 1-seeds in the Sweet 16). Regardless, again Arizona is underrated, and both Jay and Jeff picked Wisconsin to advance to the Final Four.

Of the teams remaining, and taking Kentucky out of the mix, the least likely team to get upset is Duke. Arizona is a team that is playing its best basketball right now, but the Wildcats still have Wisconsin in its path (and that is not an upset, is it?). Duke is really young, but it’s not as if Utah is filled with seasoned veterans just back from missions and all 25 years old. Duke is uber talented and an incredible offensive team and a much-improved defensive team over the past five weeks.

Other news from around the web:

  • Two great articles in the Cincinatti Enquirer, detailing the Xavier head coach Chris Mack’s relationship with Sean Miller, as well as their teams’ matchup on Thursday.

  • TJ McConnell was on the Jim Rome Show on Monday, and they discussed topics such as his relationship with Coach Miller, being compared to Aaron Craft, and wanting another shot at Wisconsin.

  • Bruce Pascoe covered the importance of this weekend for Arizona, noting:

Arizona’s four Elite Eight losses [in the last 14 years] have been decided by a total of seven points, with the Wildcats failing to make a shot on their final possession (Jason Gardner in 2003, Hassan Adams in ’05, Jamelle Horne in ’11 and Nick Johnson in ’14)
