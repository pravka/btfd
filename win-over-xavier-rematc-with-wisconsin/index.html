Bear The F*** Down

Wildcats win over Xavier, setup rematch with Wisconsin

27/Mar 2015

It wasn’t pretty, but it’s a W, and in March Madness that’s all that matters. With a game-ending 19-7 run and increased defensive intensity down the stretch, the Wildcats dispatched Xavier.

The win sets the stage for one of the most anticipated matchups of this tourney: Arizona vs Wisconsin. Some coverage from around the interwebs:
